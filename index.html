Consensus

Welcome to our innovative school, a cutting-edge educational environment powered by Linux and open-source technologies, all housed within repurposed shipping containers. Each shipping container classroom is equipped with its own dedicated Starlink Internet connection, ensuring high-speed satellite access that enhances learning experiences. This school addresses critical gaps in education, such as financial literacy, by incorporating real-world financial concepts into the curriculum while fostering digital literacy through hands-on learning. We reject standardised testing that ranks students and limits creativity, advocating instead for student autonomy and voice, ensuring every learner has agency in their education. By moving beyond outdated classroom structures reminiscent of the 1920s, we create an engaging, dynamic environment that prioritises reading comprehension over phonics alone, encouraging a love for learning in an exciting, modern context.
